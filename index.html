Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and expertise.

Why should I choose Lehigh Valley Digital over other agencies?
+

At Lehigh Valley Digital, we offer custom-coded websites and digital solutions that are tailored to your business needs at an affordable price. Unlike template-based agencies, we focus on scalable, future-proof solutions that grow with your business while delivering exceptional value. Additionally, every client is set up with their own GitHub account where they own all their code and have access to a comprehensive, easy-to-understand report that outlines every part of their code.

How are your prices so affordable without compromising quality?
+

We leverage mature technologies, streamlined processes, and a lean operational structure to keep costs low while maintaining high-quality standards. This means your investment goes directly into results, not overhead. Plus, with access to your GitHub repository and a detailed code report, you can see exactly what you're paying for‚Äîno hidden work or technical ambiguity.

What makes your approach innovative?
+

We stay ahead of the curve by adopting the latest tools, frameworks, and trends in the digital space. From advanced coding techniques to customized branding, we ensure your digital presence is modern, competitive, and effective. Our commitment to transparency, including the ability for clients to review and own their code, reflects our dedication to education and empowerment.

What does the Basic Branding Package include?
+

Our Basic Branding Package is perfect for startups looking for a cost-effective solution. It includes one logo concept, one revision, and suggestions for a basic color palette and typography to kickstart your brand identity. For businesses that choose to integrate branding with a website, we also ensure seamless access to branding assets through your GitHub repository.

What's the difference between the Standard and Premium Branding Packages?
+

The Standard Package offers more variety with three logo concepts, two revisions, a comprehensive color palette, typography, and basic brand guidelines. The Premium Package takes it further with five logo concepts, unlimited revisions, a full brand identity, marketing collateral, and a brand strategy consultation. For both, if branding elements are integrated into digital solutions, you'll have full access and control via your GitHub repository.

What's the advantage of choosing a custom-coded website?
+

Custom-coded websites are uniquely designed to reflect your brand and meet your specific needs. Unlike template-based sites, you'll have full ownership and flexibility to expand or modify your site as your business evolves. Plus, every client is provided with their own GitHub account, where all your code is stored. Along with this, you'll receive a clear, non-technical report breaking down every part of your code, ensuring you understand exactly how your website works.
